Egg Curry

Description

Egg Curry is a delicious meal that can be eaten any time of the day! Try this easy recipe out!

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Curry Block
  • Water
  • Carrots

Directions

  1. Heat up your water to 1000 degrees celsius
  2. Put a whole carrot into the water when the desired temperature is reached
  3. Put the curry block into the water immediately after adding the whole carrot
  4. Heat up another pan to 100 degrees celsius
  5. Crack open your egg on to the pan when the desired temperature is reached, do not worry about oil
  6. After the curry has cooked for 1 hour you can began plating
  7. Add a scoop of curry over rice and add your egg that you made earlier on the pan
