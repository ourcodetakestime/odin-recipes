Tonkatsu

Description

Tonkatsu is a delicious meal that can be eaten any time of the day! Try this easy recipe out!

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Flour
  • Panko
  • Egg

Directions

  1. Mix up your egg in a bowl
  2. Add your pork to the egg mix
  3. Add your egg soaked pork into your flour
  4. Add your flour and egg soaked pork back into the egg mix
  5. Add your pork into the panko
  6. Fry your pork until internal temperature of 100 degrees celsius
